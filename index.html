Abstract

Organ systems from the kidney to the brain depend on fundamental metabolic pathways that are essential to all of life.1

Recent work has demonstrated insight into the basic physical properties that allow electron transport chains to exist.4

Impacts ranging from cancer cell targeting to potential dampeners of aging have been proposed and readily investigated. Quantum biology is now used in analyzing and predicting the behaviors of more complex, intracellular systems.

Deeper understanding of disease, novel understanding of aging that can answer fundamental questions of metabolic efficiency.2,3,5
